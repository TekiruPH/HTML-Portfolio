I Simplify the Work of Teams

Sometimes, being able to simplify the work is all that matters. I try as much as possible not to add a lot of complication in work. And with the age of AI, i really think that more and more of us should embrace less complexity. Let's enjoy life. And rekindle the days when a website is this simple.

Why Am I Doing This?

I took a full stack development course in Udemy. And i need to showcase a raw HTML website without CSS. Although it does feel like the 80's calling me back. I do like the simplicity this brings. Not having to create styles. Or div classes. Or any of that React or Tailwind front end shenanigans.

I do want to learn a little better than this. And i dont think ChatGPT and Claude need prodding from me.
